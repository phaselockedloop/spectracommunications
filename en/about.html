ABOUT SPECTRA COMMUNICATIONS
Spectra Communications is a translation and localization company located in Montreal, Quebec.

Sarah Brunel, our Lead Translator and Founder graduated from Concordia University in Montreal with a BA - Specialization in Translation and a Minor in Sociology. She later returned to attend graduate-level courses in localization and project management.

Professionally, Sarah has over 11 years of experience under her belt. Working for the Government and some of Canada’s top companies as a translator has allowed her to become an expert in the Quebec, Canadian and American markets. Her extensive knowledge in a variety of specialty fields and collaboration with professionals from various industries have shaped her into a versatile translator and team-player who is client-focused, reliable, flexible and well-organized.

Sarah is also committed to continuous learning – she regularly attends continuous improvement classes, conferences and events to stay up-to-date with the law, market standards and the evolution of language and technology.
